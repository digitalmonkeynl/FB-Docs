Start enjoying your MilesWhat is even better than earning Award Miles? Of course… spending them! Flying Blue offers you an impressive number of possibilities to do so – on flights, hotels, rental cars and much more.

1On flights

With your Award Miles, you can book an award flight with AIR FRANCE, KLM or one of our many Flying Blue airline partners. The number of Miles you need depends on your destination. And remember – always check if there is a special promotion on offer in the Promo Awards@ section.

  • See all types of award tickets
  • Book your award ticket

New Promo Awards@ to choose from every month!

Each month, AIR FRANCE and KLM offer discounted award flights to a new selection of destinations. So check out our Promo Awards@ and book a flight with up to 50% off the number of Miles usually required for an award ticket.

Want to book a Promo Award@ with AIR FRANCE, KLM or one of our airline partners? Book now!

2Spend Miles on flight-related option

Extra comfort during your flight, taking more baggage or enjoying some relaxing hours in the lounge at the airport? Pick your choice and pay with Miles for any of the following services:

  • Extra baggage award
  • Upgrade award
  • Extra legroom
  • A la carte meals
  • Lounge access
  • Economy comfort
  • Seat in a row of 2
  • Shopping in the air

3Spend Miles on hotels, rental cars and other products and services

Not flying very often? Don`t worry: our Flying Blue Store offers numerous options to spend your Miles on hotel stays, car hire, gift boxes... Or how about a marathon entry ticket or a drive in a Ferrari? Just discover what`s there for you!

  • Discover all our partners

4Donate your Miles to charity

Flying Blue cares about your comfort. But not only about that. We also care about the well-being of others. Therefore, we teamed up with a number of charities to provide help all over the world. And with your Miles we can provide even more help!

Earn Miles your wayIt`s very simple: you earn Miles for flying with us. And that`s not the only way to top up your Miles balance. Even if you do not fly very often, earning Miles is still possible with many of our programme partners. Just earn them your way – in the air or on the ground!

1On flights

You earn Award Miles when flying with AIR FRANCE, KLM or any of our other airline partners. It's very easy to earn them: you simply mention your Flying Blue number while booking. And if you forgot to do so, you can still claim your missing Miles within 6 months. Flights with AIR FRANCE, KLM and our SkyTeam partners also earn you Level Miles. They will help you maintain and upgrade your membership level. For more information about the specific accrual schemes of AIR FRANCE, KLM, and our other airline partners, please visit the airline partners overview.

  • Want to book a flight? Go to our booking tool
Miles Finder
Use the Miles Finder to see how many Miles you earn on your next flight.

2By using your Flying Blue – American Express Card

Earn Miles while paying for your purchases! You will earn Award Miles for every transaction made with your Flying Blue – American Express Card. On top of this, members with a Flying Blue – American Express Gold or Platinum Card will earn Level Miles for transactions with AIR FRANCE and KLM. Each year American Express Card holders will also receive extra qualifying flights from American Express. This means that you will not only maintain your current membership level easier, but also reach your next membership level quicker!

  • Check out all Flying Blue – American Express cards

U kunt uw Award Miles-saldo ook verhogen met behulp van Share&Fly®. Kaarthouders binnen dezelfde American Express-familie kunnen met deze gebruiksvriendelijke optie kosteloos Award Miles delen tussen de hoofdkaarthouder en andere kaarthouders binnen hetzelfde account.

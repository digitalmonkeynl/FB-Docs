Earn Miles and enjoy exclusive benefitsFlying Blue is your ideal travelling companion – earn Miles while flying and spend them on flights, hotel stays, car rentals and much more. And you know what is best? Joining Flying Blue is completely free!

Get rewarded for flying with us

Every time you book an applicable flight with AIR FRANCE, KLM or any of our airline partners, Flying Blue lets you earn Miles. All you have to do is mention your Flying Blue number each time you book a flight or make use of the services of one of our partners.

There are two types of Miles you can earn: Award Miles and Level Miles.

  • Award Miles can be spent on tickets, upgrades, Promo Awards@ or products and services from our 130 partners.
  • Level Miles bring you to a higher Flying Blue membership level that offers extra services and benefits. With a higher membership level, for example, you're entitled to a bonus of Award Miles for applicable flights.

Get the recognition you deserve

Flying Blue has four different levels of membership: Ivory, Silver, Gold and Platinum. As you travel more and more with us, we reward your loyalty by offering more Miles, additional services and ever-increasing benefits that are yours to enjoy. Simply mention or show your Flying Blue card and you’ll experience smoother, easier and more pleasant travels. It's our way of showing how much we value you.

American Express, reliable service since 1850

American Express is one of the best-known financial service providers in the world. The organisation, founded in 1850 as an express company, has a rich history. It handed out the first credit card in 1958 – made from paper! Now, more than 50 years later, American Express has almost worldwide coverage and an exceptionally strong reputation.

With an American Express Card you will travel safely and carefree, while enjoying an excellent service and collecting Award Miles with every purchase.

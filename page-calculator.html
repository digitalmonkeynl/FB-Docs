Please note the following conditions regarding spending Miles
  • The rules regarding spending Award Miles are subject to change.
  • Results are merely indicative; no rights can be derived from these results.
  • Awards that combine the purchase of tickets from more than one airline are approved only when these tickets were purchased for flights operated by Flying Blue and SkyTeam airline partners; otherwise, separate award bookings will need to be requested and approved.
  • The number of Miles listed as being required for award flights applies to a round-trip ticket (a one-way ticket would require half this number of Miles).
  • The number of Miles listed as being required for an upgrade applies to a one-way flight. Class restrictions may apply to upgrades.
  • All award travel is limited to published routes.
  • You can book your award flight online or by calling the Flying Blue Service Centre.
  • Please check your Flying Blue news updates or the Flying Blue partner pages for applicable blackout dates.

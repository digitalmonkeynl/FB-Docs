Earn Miles your way

It’s very simple: you earn Miles for flying with us. And that`s not the only way to top up your Miles balance. Even if you do not fly very often, earning Miles is still possible with many of our programme partners. Just earn them your way – in the air or on the ground!

On Flights

You earn Award Miles when flying with AIR FRANCE, KLM or any of our other airline partners.

It's very easy to earn them: you simply mention your Flying Blue number while booking. And if you forgot to do so, you can still claim your missing Miles within 6 months. Flights with AIR FRANCE, KLM and our SkyTeam partners also earn you Level Miles. They will help you maintain and upgrade your membership level.

By using your AIR FRANCE KLM – AMERICAN EXPRESS Card

You earn Award Miles when flying with AIR FRANCE, KLM or any of our other airline partners.

Earn Miles while paying for your purchases! You will earn Award Miles for every transaction made with your AIR FRANCE KLM – AMERICAN EXPRESS Card. On top of this, members with a AIR FRANCE KLM – AMERICAN EXPRESS Gold or Platinum Card will earn Level Miles for transactions with AIR FRANCE and KLM. Each year American Express Card holders will also receive extra qualifying flights from American Express. This means that you will not only maintain your current membership level easier, but also reach your next membership level quicker!

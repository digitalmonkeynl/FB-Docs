A world of benefits

Being a Flying Blue member means that you can enjoy certain benefits: benefits as a member of our programme, benefits on flights and also benefits from our partners. These partners, both in the air and on the ground, all have many great offers of which members can take advantage. Our airline partners provide a host of permanent benefits, so have a look on the specific airline partner page for more information. Our partners on the ground regularly offer exclusive promotions on hotel stays, car rentals, or financial services, for example. Check out the individual partner pages and find out more.
